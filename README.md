## Echora

**Echora** is a music app designed to bring people together through music. Discover new songs, connect with other music lovers, and share your favorite tracks in a vibrant community.
It is a platform where music becomes social interaction in real time

### Features
- Artists post audio and listeners interact with it instantly this turning passive consumption of music into conversation
- Discover and play music
- Connect with a community of music fans
- Share and discuss your favorite songs


### Tools
- **Frontend:** React Native (with Expo)
- **Backend:** ExpressJS,postgresql

### Getting Started
1. Clone the repository
2. Install dependencies with `npm install`
3. Start the app with `npx expo start`
